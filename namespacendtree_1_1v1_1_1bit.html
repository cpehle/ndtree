Maximum representable unsigned integer value in no_bits.

Formula: 2^{n} - 1

Since 2^{n} might not fit in the integer type it uses the following relation: 2^{n} - 1 = 2^{n - 1} * 2 - 1 = 2^{n - 1} + (2^{n - 1} - 1)
